Our Story

A Phoenix Institution
Since 1974

For over 50 years, My Mother's Restaurant has been serving the kind of food that makes you feel like you're back in your grandma's kitchen. It's not about modern twists or fancy presentations — it's about honest, homestyle American cooking done right.

Our slow-roasted prime rib, tender pot roast that falls right off the bone, carved turkey dinner with all the sides, and our famous homemade bread with honey butter keep families coming back generation after generation.
